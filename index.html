About Me

My goal is to establish the computational foundations of effective human-AI collaboration. I'm excited about tackling questions around:

  • Scalable measurement of the value and risks of AI in collaborative work
  • Efficient learning from real-world interaction and feedback
  • Designing new forms of collaboration and oversight

I recently completed my PhD in Machine Learning at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was advised by Ameet Talwalkar. I have been selected as a "ML and Systems Rising Star" and "Rising Star in Data Science", and my research was supported by a CMU Presidential Fellowship and NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. I have also interned at OpenHands (with Graham Neubig), NYU (with He He), and Microsoft Research (with Q. Vera Liao and Jenn Wortman Vaughan).

Recruiting PhD students and postdocs (Fall 2027)! I'm excited to be joining UIUC as an Assistant Professor in Fall 2027! I will be recruiting PhD students and postdocs this cycle. PhD applicants can apply through either ECE or CS.
